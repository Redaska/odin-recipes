Tajin with eggs and Meat-balls

Description

Moroccan cuisine is a fusion of Mediterranean, Arab, and African flavors, featuring spices, herbs, vegetables, and meats. Popular dishes include tagines, couscous, and harira soup, often accompanied by mint tea and pastries.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • 4-6 medium-sized tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. In a tajine or a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and chopped bell peppers and continue to sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper, and stir everything together.
  4. Cover the tajine or skillet and let the tomato mixture simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft and tender.
  5. Once the tomato mixture is cooked, use a spoon to create small wells in the mixture and crack an egg into each well.
  6. Cover the tajine or skillet again and let the eggs cook in the tomato mixture for about 5-10 minutes, or until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your liking.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley and serve hot with crusty bread or pita.
